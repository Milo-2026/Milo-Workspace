## Recording Tips

1. **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light
2. **Audio:** Clear, not too fast
3. **Energy:** Confident, helpful, not salesy
4. **Length:** Stay under 2 minutes
5. **B-roll:** Add roofing footage for visual interest
