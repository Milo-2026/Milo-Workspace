# Side Quest Studios - AI Content Strategy

## Mission
Help skeptical business owners understand AI, overcome their fears, and take their first steps toward AI adoption - one small step at a time.

## Target Audience
- Business owners who are overwhelmed by AI
- Haven't adopted AI tools yet
- Don't know where to start
- Skeptical of AI claims
- Need simple, practical guidance

## Content Pillars

### 1. AI Explained (No Jargon)
Simple breakdowns of AI tools and concepts. No technical jargon - just what it does and why it matters.

### 2. Start Small
One task at a time approach. Don't overwhelm them. Show them how to start with ONE small task.

### 3. Fear Check
Address common concerns:
- "AI will replace me"
- "It's too complicated"
- "I don't have time"
- "It costs too much"
- "My business is different"

### 4. Database Basics
Why data matters, what to track, and how to start - even with spreadsheets.

### 5. Real Results
Case studies, success stories, ROI examples from real small businesses.

## Key Messages

1. **Start small** - You don't need to transform everything at once
2. **AI is a tool** - It won't replace you, it will amplify you
3. **You already have data** - Here's how to use it
4. **It's not as hard as you think** - We'll show you step by step
5. **Other small businesses are doing it** - Here's how

---

## Tone Guidelines
- ✅ Encouraging, not condescending
- ✅ Practical, not theoretical
- ✅ Honest about limitations
- ✅ Real results, not hype
- ✅ One step at a time

- ❌ Don't use jargon
- ❌ Don't overwhelm with options
- ❌ Don't promise magic results
- ❌ Don't make it sound easy (it's a journey)
