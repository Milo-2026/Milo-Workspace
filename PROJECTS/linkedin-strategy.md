## LinkedIn Content Strategy

### Posting Frequency
- **AddOnQuote:** 3-4 posts/week
- **Side Quest:** 3-4 posts/week  
- **Sheet It Now:** 3-4 posts/week

### Best Posting Times
- Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday: 8-10 AM EST
- Wednesday: 12-1 PM EST

### Content Mix (Per Brand)

**AddOnQuote (Contractor Audience)**
- 40% Industry tips and education
- 30% Case studies and success stories
- 20% Product features and updates
- 10% Behind the scenes

**Side Quest (Developer/Indie Hacker Audience)**
- 35% Build in public updates
- 30% Technical insights
- 25% Product development
- 10% Team culture

**Sheet It Now (Finance/Accounting Audience)**
- 35% Privacy and security
- 30% Use cases and tutorials
- 25% Efficiency tips
- 10% Customer stories

## Content Formats That Work on LinkedIn

### 1. Text Posts (150-300 words)
```
[Hook - 1-2 sentences]

[Body - 3-5 paragraphs]

[Call to action - 1 sentence]

#hashtags (3-5 relevant)
```

### 2. Carousel Posts (5-10 slides)
- Slide 1: Title
- Slides 2-8: Tips or steps
- Slide 9: Summary
- Slide 10: CTA

### 3. Document Posts (PDF carousel)
- Industry guide
- Checklists
- Templates
