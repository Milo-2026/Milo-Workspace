## Quick Reference

### The Pitch (2 minutes)

**Problem:** Families struggle to coordinate grocery shopping. Existing apps share lists but don't solve coordination, context, or budget.

**Solution:** A family-first grocery app with real-time sync, smart organization, and running cost estimates.

**Market:** 40M US households, willing to pay $2-5/month.

**Differentiation:** Family coordination + budget awareness + smart defaults.

---

### MVP Feature Set (V1)

**Must-Have:**
1. ✅ Create/edit/delete grocery lists
2. ✅ Add items with quantity and category
3. ✅ Check off items
4. ✅ Share list with family members
5. ✅ Sync across devices
6. ✅ Quick add (tap + to add)
7. ✅ Store categories (Produce, Dairy, Meat, etc.)
8. ✅ Voice input
9. ✅ Offline support

**Estimated Timeline:** 7 weeks

---

### Key Screens

1. **Home** - List of household lists with member avatars
2. **List Detail** - Items organized by category, checkable
3. **Add Item** - Quick modal with autocomplete
4. **Share** - Invite family via link or email

---

### Technical Approach

- **Frontend:** React Native (iOS + Android)
- **Backend:** Firebase (Auth + Realtime Database)
- **Sync:** Offline-first with automatic reconciliation
- **Hosting:** Vercel for web components

---

### Design Highlights

- **Color:** Fresh green (#22C55E) primary, category accents
- **Philosophy:** "Calm Clarity" - simple, thumb-friendly, scan-optimized
- **Accessibility:** VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, high contrast support
