## Part 1: The Lead Magnet Strategy

### What We Have
- **Lead Magnet:** Free "Profit Resource" checklist (avg recovery: $800/job)
- **Conversion Path:** Landing page → Email capture → Nurture sequence → Offer

### The Miami Test ($100/month)

**Week 1: Setup**
- Facebook Ads targeting Miami roofers
- Budget: $100/month (~ $3.33/day)
- Ad copy: "Miami roofers: Stop leaving $800/job on the table"
- Landing page: https://guide.addonquote.app/
- Email follow-up: 3-touch sequence over 7 days

**Week 2-4: Optimize**
- A/B test ad variations
- Measure: CPM, CTR, Email capture rate, Demo signup rate
- Target metrics:
  - CPM < $15
  - CTR > 1.5%
  - Email capture > 8%
  - Demo signup > 15% of captures

**If it works:**
- Scale budget to $500/month
- Expand to other cities (Orlando, Tampa, Jacksonville)

**If it doesn't:**
- Test different ad creative
- Try Google Ads (high intent searches)
- Pivot to organic only
