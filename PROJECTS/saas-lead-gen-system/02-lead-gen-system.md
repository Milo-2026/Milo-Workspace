## 7. Automation Workflows

### Workflow 1: Lead Capture → Email Sequence (AddOnQuote)
```
[Website Form]
      ↓
[Webhook to Zapier]
      ↓
[Create contact in HubSpot]
      ↓
[Send welcome email]
      ↓
[Add to 5-day email sequence]
      ↓
[If no reply after 5 days → Add to re-engagement sequence]
```

**Tools:** HubSpot Forms → Zapier → HubSpot → Gmail

**Time to build:** 2 hours
**Cost:** $0 (HubSpot Free + Zapier Free)

### Workflow 2: Trial Signup → Onboarding (All Products)
```
[Trial Signup]
      ↓
[Send welcome email with onboarding steps]
      ↓
[Track product usage (Mixpanel/Amplitude)]
      ↓
[If inactive for 3 days → Send re-engagement email]
      ↓
[If still inactive → Add to "win-back" campaign]
```

**Tools:** Your app → Customer.io/SendGrid → Analytics

**Time to build:** 4 hours
**Cost:** $0-50/mo

### Workflow 3: Content Download → Newsletter (Side Quest)
```
[Content Download Form]
      ↓
[Add to ConvertKit/Mailchimp]
      ↓
[Send content + newsletter signup CTA]
      ↓
[2x/month: Send newsletter with project updates]
      ↓
[Quarterly: Project inquiry CTA]
```

**Tools:** ConvertKit Free + WordPress/Lemma

**Time to build:** 1 hour
**Cost:** $0

### Workflow 4: Review Monitoring → Testimonial Collection
```
[Google/Product Hunt review alert]
      ↓
[If positive → Send testimonial request email]
      ↓
[Collect testimonial + permission for marketing]
      ↓
[Add to testimonial library]
      ↓
[Feature in marketing materials]
```

**Tools:** Google Alerts + Typeform + Notion

**Time to build:** 30 minutes
**Cost:** $0
