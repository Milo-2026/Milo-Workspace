## 📦 The Three SaaS Products

### AddOnQuote (Roofing SaaS)
- **Focus:** Roofing estimate software for contractors
- **Target Audience:** Roofing companies, insurance adjusters
- **Priority:** 🔴 HIGHEST (Revenue-focused)
- **Lead Gen Strategy:** Cold outreach + content marketing

### Sheet It Now (PDF → Excel)
- **Focus:** Document conversion tool
- **Target Audience:** Accountants, data entry, small business
- **Priority:** 🟡 MEDIUM (Product-led growth)
- **Lead Gen Strategy:** SEO + content + product usage

### Side Quest Studios (Studio Positioning)
- **Focus:** Indie game development studio
- **Target Audience:** Indie game devs, gamers
- **Priority:** 🟢 LOWER (Brand building)
- **Lead Gen Strategy:** Build-in-public + community

## 📊 The Three Systems

### 1. Trend Analysis System
**Purpose:** Identify SaaS opportunities before competitors

**Daily Routine (30 min):**
- [ ] Check Google Trends (10 min)
- [ ] Scan Product Hunt (10 min)
- [ ] Review Indie Hackers (10 min)

**Weekly Output:**
- [ ] 10+ trends identified
- [ ] 2-3 opportunities scored
- [ ] 1 deep-dive analysis

**Deliverable:** `01-trend-analysis-system.md`

---

### 2. Lead Generation System
**Purpose:** Capture and convert leads across all products

**Key Channels:**
- [ ] Email outreach (Apollo.io + GMass)
- [ ] Lead magnets (PDF guides, templates)
- [ ] Content marketing (blog, social)
- [ ] Paid ads (Google - Week 2+)

**Key Metrics:**
- [ ] 100+ leads/week
- [ ] 10% conversion rate
- [ ] <$15 cost per lead

**Deliverable:** `02-lead-gen-system.md`

---

### 3. Content & Brand System
**Purpose:** Build brand awareness and authority

**Content Pillars:**
- [ ] AddOnQuote: Roofing tips, estimate guides, industry news
- [ ] Sheet It Now: Tutorials, productivity, use cases
- [ ] Side Quest: Build-in-public, dev tips, studio culture

**Weekly Output:**
- [ ] 8-12 blog posts
- [ ] 60-80 social posts
- [ ] 4 newsletters
- [ ] 4-8 videos

**Deliverable:** `03-content-system.md`
