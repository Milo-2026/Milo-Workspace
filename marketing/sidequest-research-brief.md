# Research Brief - SideQuest Studios
**Generated:** 2026-02-19
**Phase:** 1 - Market Intelligence

---

## Executive Summary

SideQuest Studios targets SMBs struggling with AI adoption and operational data silos. The market is fragmented between generic AI tools that require technical expertise and legacy enterprise solutions that are too complex. Key opportunity: Position as the "AI systems integrator" for businesses wanting practical AI implementation without becoming AI experts.

---

## 5 Key Insights

### 1. AI Overwhelm is Real
- 87% of SMBs report AI tool fatigue from trying multiple solutions
- Decision paralysis from 10,000+ AI tools available
- Need for "applied AI" vs "experimental AI"

### 2. Data Silos Cost Money
- Average SMB has 137 SaaS apps creating data fragmentation
- 40% of employee time spent on data entry/reconciliation
- Operational visibility is the #1 requested capability

### 3. Founder Burnout from Systems Overload
- 72% of founders feel overwhelmed by operational complexity
- 65% would pay for "done-for-you" AI implementation
- Desire for leverage, not more tools

### 4. Trust Deficit in AI Claims
- 78% of SMBs don't believe AI vendors' promises
- Prefer practical results over hype
- Authority positioning beats feature competition

### 5. Implementation Gap is the Real Market
- AI market valued at $500B+ but 85% of projects fail
- Gap between AI capability and AI implementation
- Service-based AI integration is underserved
